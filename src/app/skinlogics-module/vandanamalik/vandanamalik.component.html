Dr. Vandana Malik

Dr. Vandana malik is a certified Dermatologist and has completed her MBBS from Muzaffarnagar medical college and MD (Dermatology, Venereology and Leprosy) from YSMU, Armenia. In her vast experience of 6 years she was associated with various prestigious organisations like BPS hospital, RML hospital and DERMAWAVE SKIN CENTRE etc. She is well versed with the various problems related to skin, hair and nails. She also deals with problems related to oral mucosa and genitals, including sexually transmitted diseases and leprosy. She has a vast knowledge and experience with various dermatological procedures involving the skin, nail, hair and mucosa, including electrocautery, various lasers for skin resurfacing, skin lightening, skin rejuvenation, chemical peels, skin biopsy, microderma abrasion, microneedling, prp and various other dermatosurgical procedures. She is also well versed various other surgical and non surgical techniques for treating various skin and hair problems. She is also trained in use of Dermoscopy and Trichoscopy. With special interests in anti -aging and aesthetic medicine, she did her fellowship from university Medicine Greifswald, Germany. She has gained professional training in injectables like Anti Wrinkle Injection, Fillers and Thread lifts and permanent make up for Eyebrows, lios and BB glow for face. Dr. Vandana patiently understands the problems of the patients and is experienced and well versed in treating a wide array of dermatological conditions. She is always eager to learn the newer therapies available to address the problems of the patients better.
