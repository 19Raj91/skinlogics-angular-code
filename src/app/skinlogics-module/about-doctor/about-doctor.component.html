Dr. Sumaira Bhat

MBBS, Diploma in Clinical Dermatology (UK), MSc. - Clinical Dermatology, Dermatologist

Dr. Sumaira Bhat is a Dermatologist and Cosmetologist working in Noida. She has done her MBBS from Rajiv Gandhi University Karnataka and Masters in Dermatology from Cardiff university (UK).She has done her fellowships in aesthetics like lasers botox fillers etc. from UK. Dr. Sumaira Bhat possesses both national and international experience of over 12 years in the field of clinical dermatology and aesthetics. She has worked with the best hospital chains in UK, Gulf and India.

She is a world-class expert in treating scars, anti-ageing treatment and hair loss and balances science with art to give her patients fabulous lasting result. Besides that, she is dealing with cosmetology as well. She practices general dermatology with special interest in hair disorders, aesthetic and anti-aging medicine. She is recognized nationally and internationally for her innovative work with skin lasers/RF, cosmetic dermatology and facial rejuvenation techniques. . She has been awarded as corona warrior by state affairs in helping people during covid times with skin problems. She is an member of Association of dermatologists(UK) and IADVL(India).

Her aesthetic magic has been the silent reason behind the captivating beauty across the borders. She has enhanced and perfected thousands of beautiful faces, alluring tresses and helped her patients discover the best of their natural beauty. Dr. Sumaira believes in achieving the best beautiful outcomes by combining the scientific advances in aesthetics, her excellent skills in both clinical dermatology and aesthetics , and keen eye for aesthetics to achieve natural results.
