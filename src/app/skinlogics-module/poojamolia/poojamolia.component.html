Dr. Pooja Moliya

Dr Pooja Moliya is Dermatologist and Cosmetologist working in Noida. She has done her MBBS from Pandit Deendayal Upadhyay Medical college Rajkot and Masters in Dermatology from Smt NHL Municipal Medical college Ahmedabad. She has very good experience in diagnosis and treatment of various common and rare Dermatological conditions owing to vast exposure received in government set up in last 4 years. She is a member of IADVL (India). She practices general dermatology with special interest in hair and nail disorders, aesthetic procedure and anti-aging medicine. She has good skill in performing various dermatosurgery like vitiligo surgeries, electrocautery and radio frequency abalation, various skin and nail biopsies, nail surgeries, scar revision surgeries etc. She is also expert in various cosmetic procedure like PRP, microneedling, chemical peels, acne scar management, mesotherapy, LHR and various laser.
